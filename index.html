NeVRMind

A biofeedback driven VR meditation designed to help focus and relax patients who will be undergoing psychedelic assisted therapy. Developed for Psyber, a subsidiary company of ATAI Life Sciences. The experience runs on mobile VR headsets such as the Oculus Quest.

Off the shelf EEG devices such as the Muse are enhanced with propriety machine learning tools to better recognize and reward states of calm focus in patients.
