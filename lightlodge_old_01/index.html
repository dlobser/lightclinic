Light Lodge

We offer ketamine assisted therapy (KAT) using virtual reality (VR) to enhance the experience. The company aims to make KAT more accessible and effective by offering group ceremonies that provide a mediated social experience. The company can bring its VR setup to any location with enough space, making it highly mobile. It appears that the use of VR has been successful in improving the KAT experience, as clients have reported deep insights, awe, and catharsis. Overall, Light Lodge aims to enable powerful and awe-inspiring aesthetic experiences to improve the depth and emotional impact of KAT.

Group KAT Work

The benefits of group therapy:

  • Support: Group therapy provides a supportive environment where individuals can share their experiences and feelings with others who are going through similar challenges. This can help individuals feel less alone and more understood.
  • Perspective: Participating in group therapy allows individuals to gain new perspectives and insights from others' experiences and perspectives. This can help individuals feel less isolated and more connected to others.
  • Empathy: Group therapy can foster empathy and understanding among group members as they share and discuss their experiences. This can help individuals feel more connected and less isolated.
  • Personal growth: Group therapy can facilitate personal growth and self-awareness as individuals reflect on their own experiences and behaviors in the context of the group.
